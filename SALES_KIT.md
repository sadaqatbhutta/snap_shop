## Demo Checklist (15-20 minutes)

### Before the demo

- Prepare a demo business account
- Add 10-20 FAQs in AI settings
- Create 2 segments (e.g., VIP, inactive customers)
- Create 2 templates (promo + reminder)
- Keep one scheduled broadcast ready
- Confirm all channels shown in your package are available

### Demo flow

1. **Conversations**
   - Show incoming message
   - Show AI response
   - Show human escalation and manual reply

2. **AI Settings**
   - Show FAQs and business context
   - Explain confidence/escalation behavior

3. **Segments**
   - Show segment creation with channel/tags
   - Explain targeted audience logic

4. **Templates**
   - Show reusable message template creation

5. **Broadcasts**
   - Select segment and template
   - Schedule broadcast and explain send-now flow

6. **Analytics**
   - Show conversation trend
   - Show channel distribution
   - Show intent insights

7. **Integrations**
   - Show webchat widget snippet
   - Explain "copy script and go live"

### Close questions

- How many incoming leads do you receive daily?
- How many go unreplied after business hours?
- How often do you run re-engagement campaigns?
- Would reducing missed leads by 15-25% impact monthly sales?

### Call to action

- Offer Growth Pro as default recommendation
- Offer 7-day implementation and first campaign setup
